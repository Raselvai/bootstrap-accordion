This program teaches grammar. Once a child grasps grammar, vocabulary follows.

Knowing grammar means knowing how to apply every verb in 36 applications (tenses, gender, singular/plural etc.) Therefore, after getting the Einei Haloshon app, it’s so much easier to understand what every word means within a perek or pasuk.

Absolutely. By knowing the rules, he will immediately grasp a part of the definition. For instance, when an Einei Haloshon student learns the word ילבש (to dress) for the first time, he knows that somebody (male + singular) will be (future) doing something here. He simply has to find out what לבש means, and he’s got the full picture. With this skill, he will improve his vocabulary every time he learns a new word.
